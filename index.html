Campus Life


Junior School Hub

Was constructed in 2018.

The Junior School Hub is a 2-storey building constructed in 2018 consisting of 15 classrooms which are used to teach Year levels Kindagarten to Year 4. This magnificent building has a balcony and also an indoor slide which is used to entertain students.



SPACE Building

Was constructed in 2019.

The SPACE Building consists of a basketball court made from a one of a kind material in the southern hemisphere and a massive stage used for performences. There are three classes located on the mezzanine floor and an office area for the HPE staff.



Early Learning Center

Was constructed in 2004.

The Early Learning Center is a place of growth and nurturing at Brindabell Christian College. Children from the age of 3 yrs old can be enrolled for a daycare program where they will be takne care of by our staff.

Achievements

Debate Competitions

Placed 2nd place in the ACT Christian Schools Debating Competitions

We are incredibly proud of all our students who competed in this competition. They spent countless hours preparing their speeches and it is great to see the future leaders of the world put their skills to good use.
Global Studies Competition

Placed 3rd in The Inaugural Global Studies Competition

The Global Studies Class of Year 12 got assigned the country South Korea and had to research the government of the country and address ways to improve it. We are incredibly proud of these students.
Australian National Eisteddfod

Recieved a gold award in the Australian National Eisteddfod's Intermediate Category

On Saturday the 12th May, the Senior Band went to ANU Hall to perform for the ANE. They played the title track of How to Train Your Dragon and The Mandolorian. We are extremely proud of all these students for sharing their talents with other people.
